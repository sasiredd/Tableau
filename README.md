# PROJECT:Tableau
Beverage Sales Performance Analysis (Tableau Dashboard)

• Analyzed 10,000+ beverage sales records across the United States to identify trends in revenue, profit, and product performance

• Designed interactive Tableau dashboards to visualize company-wise revenue, category performance, and regional sales distribution

• Performed data exploration on product categories, customer locations, and time-based sales patterns to uncover business insights

• Identified top-performing brands such as Coca-Cola, Dr. Pepper, and Pepsi based on revenue contribution

• Analyzed category-wise sales trends, highlighting high-demand products like Green Tea, Herbal Tea, and Wine

• Evaluated regional performance, discovering high-revenue states including California, Texas, and Florida

• Detected year-over-year sales trends, including a decline in revenue post-2022

• Provided business recommendations to improve marketing strategies, optimize costs, and enhance regional sales performance
